Saipan's Must Try Restaurants

Food is something you always must experience when you travel, so in this article, I would like to give you my
top picks on where to dine when you are visiting Saipan.

Best Places to Dine:

  1. Country House
  2. Java Joe's
  3. Majesty Chinese Restaurant
  4. Bistro Boyz

Country House. | Garapan, Saipan

Country House serves the best Loco Moco dish on the island. They have an open bar
and a great view of the Cooks in action. What I like most is how the interior establishes a country side vibe.

What I love:

  • The Loco Moco dish.
  • Dressed-up waiters and waitresses.
  • Amazing service and environment.
